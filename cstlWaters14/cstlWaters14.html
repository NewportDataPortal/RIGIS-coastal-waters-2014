Description:
Abstract:
This dataset represents coastal waters and the coastline of Rhode Island, as well as portions of neighboring Connecticut, Massachusetts, and New York. Includes Narragansett Bay, salt ponds, and tributaries.
Purpose:
This data represents Rhode Island coastal waters and its coastline, and is intended for cartographic purposes.

Process_Step:
Process_Description:
CT boundary was used to erase land areas leaving a matching CT coastline. The MA boundary was then used to erase land areas leaving a matching MA coastline. Data boundary was then extended past Block Island and into surrounding MA and CT coastal waters. Added a new attibute field called 'Water,' and dissolved Esturaries_IWQMA12 dataset based on "Water = Yes." NY shoreline boundary was then used to erase land areas leaving a matching NY coastline around Fisher Island. Removed small NY Islands from the Coastal Water dataset by heads-up digitizing from ArcGIS Online World_Imagery.
